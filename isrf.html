ISRF 2026

International Student Research Forum under ICFAEMH 2026

ISRF 2026 is an international platform dedicated to students to present their research through oral and poster presentations.

This forum aims to foster academic exchange, innovation, and collaboration among undergraduate and postgraduate students globally.

Selected papers will be published in student proceedings with ISSN.
