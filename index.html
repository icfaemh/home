ICFAEMH 2026

International Conference on Emerging Trends in Food, Agroindustry, Environmental, Molecular, and Health Sciences

Theme:
Transformative Research and Global Collaboration in Food, Agroindustry, Environmental, Molecular, and Health Sciences for Sustainable Development and Academic Excellence

ICFAEMH 2026 provides an ideal academic platform for researchers to present the latest research findings and emerging technologies in food, agroindustry, environmental, molecular, and health sciences.

The conference includes various symposium topics covering interdisciplinary fields and aims to foster collaboration among global researchers.

Accepted papers will be submitted for possible inclusion in international conference proceedings (ISSN).
